More Complexity?

There's something to say about the simplicity of my website: its efficient. Sure, I could make it look better, but it would function the same. I could use jQuery or Bootstrap, but those are bulky. This pure efficiency allows my website the ability to be loaded and viewed from any device anywhere. Huge libraries with huge amounts of functionality that ultimately goes unused should not be used on websites that could be much smaller otherwise.

Simplicity

That's not to say that websites can't look good without huge front-end frameworks holding them up. Just use some *gasp* custom CSS when you need things to look spiffy. And when you can't seem to quite figure out it out, just remember: nobody really cares. People will probably remember your website because its fairly unique. Just let you do you.
